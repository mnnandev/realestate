About This Property

A stunning pre-war loft conversion in the heart of Tribeca, one of Manhattan's most sought-after neighborhoods. This exceptional residence occupies the entire 4th floor of a landmarked cast-iron building and has been meticulously renovated to blend original industrial character with refined modern living.

Soaring 12-foot ceilings, exposed brick walls, and original wide-plank hardwood floors create an atmosphere of rare authenticity. The open-plan great room is flooded with natural light through oversized factory windows, offering breathtaking views of the Hudson River and the iconic Manhattan skyline.
