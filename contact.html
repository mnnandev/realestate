Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How quickly can you find me a home?
Most of our buyers are under contract within 30–60 days of beginning their search, depending on market conditions and the specificity of their criteria. We also have access to off-market listings that can speed this up significantly.
Do you work with buyers relocating from abroad?
Absolutely. A significant portion of our clients are international buyers or US expats returning home. We guide you through every legal, financial, and logistical aspect of purchasing US residential property as a foreign national.
What are your fees as a buyer's agent?
In most cases, our buyer's agent fee is covered by the seller as part of the transaction — meaning you benefit from full-service representation at no direct cost to you. We'll walk you through current market practices in your specific city.
Can you help me sell my current home too?
Yes — we work with sellers across all 5 of our markets. Our listing services include professional staging consultation, architectural photography, targeted digital marketing, and access to our broad network of qualified buyers.
How do off-market listings work?
Through our network of developers, attorneys, and fellow brokers, we often learn of exceptional properties before they're publicly listed. These "quiet" listings give our clients a genuine first-mover advantage in competitive markets.
Which cities do you operate in?
We currently serve buyers and sellers in New York (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Westchester), Miami (Brickell, Coconut Grove, South Beach, Coral Gables), Los Angeles (Beverly Hills, Hollywood Hills, Venice), Texas (Austin, Houston), and Chicago (Lincoln Park, Gold Coast, River North).
