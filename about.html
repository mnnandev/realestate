About Luxe Realty

We Don't Just Sell Homes — We Transform Lives

Founded in 2010 by Alexandra Monroe, Luxe Realty began with a simple belief: that buying or selling a home should feel extraordinary, not exhausting. What started as a boutique Manhattan agency has grown into one of the most trusted residential real estate firms operating across the United States.

Today, our team of 18 licensed specialists serves buyers and sellers across New York, Miami, Los Angeles, Texas, and Chicago — always with the same commitment to white-glove service, radical transparency, and results that exceed expectations.

We are proud to be ranked in the top 1% of US residential agents by transaction volume five years running, and even prouder of the hundreds of families who now call their dream homes home because of us.

— Alexandra Monroe, Founder & Principal Broker
